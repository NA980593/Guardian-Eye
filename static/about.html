About Us

Guardian Eye is dedicated to combating the rising threat of sextortion, a form of cybercrime that preys on vulnerable individuals, especially teenagers. Our mission is to empower teens with the knowledge and tools they need to recognize, avoid, and report sextortion attempts.

Our Purpose

Sextortion has become an alarming issue in recent years, with many teens unaware of the risks associated with their online interactions. Guardian Eye aims to provide a safe space where teens can find resources and support to protect themselves from this growing threat.

Who We Help

Teens at Risk: Individuals who may be targeted due to their online activity.

Parents and Guardians: Those seeking tools and knowledge to safeguard their children from digital exploitation.

Our Goals

At Guardian Eye, we strive to create a secure, proactive environment where teens can report incidents of sextortion and receive immediate assistance. Our platform integrates a variety of features aimed at empowering users and protecting their privacy.

Key Features
  • Chat Monitoring: Scans incoming messages for harmful or coercive content.
  • AI Analysis: Utilizes the Gemini API to detect suspicious language patterns.
  • Real-Time Alerts: Notifies users of potential threats and provides immediate advice.
  • Guardian Notifications: Alerts parents if a risk is detected.
  • Safety Tips: Offers guidance on handling sextortion attempts securely.
  • AI Chatbot: Provides real-time, compassionate advice for victims or individuals at risk.
Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a safer online environment for vulnerable groups and raising awareness about the dangers of sextortion. Through our comprehensive resources and support systems, we aim to empower teens and protect their digital well-being.

Get Involved

If you are a teen in need of support, a parent looking for resources, or someone interested in contributing to our mission, please reach out to us. Together, we can make a difference in the fight against sextortion.
